Lakdi Ghana Refined Oil
Oil is extracted from pure oilseeds in a scientific way at natural temperature. While heat is necessarily released during the pressing process, the temperature may not exceed 30 °C (86 °Fahrenheit) Refined oil is extracted at very high temperatures. And then heated in steam bath at the temperature of 110°C to 200°C(230°Fahrenheit and 392°Fahrenheit)
The natural ingredients, vitamins, proteins and fragrance of the oil remain intact. Oil production at high temperatures destroys the vitamins, proteins and natural aromas in the oil.
No chemicals or extracts are used for oil refining Chemicals like Hexane, Gasoline, Sodium, Bleaching powder etc. are used for refining of refined oil.
Cold pressed oil does not cause Waat (flatulence), Pitta (bile), cough related diseases. Refined oil causes Waat (flatulence), Pitta (bile),cough related diseases.
Cold pressed oil does not cause heart disease, cancer, arthritis and digestive diseases. Cold Pressed Oil increases HDL (High Density Lipoprotein) cholesterol level and reduces LDL (Low Density Lipoprotein) cholesterol levels. Refined oil causes heart disease, cancer, arthritis and digestive diseases. Refined Oil increases LDL (Low Density Lipoprotein) cholesterol level and reduces HDL (High Density Lipoprotein) cholesterol levels
